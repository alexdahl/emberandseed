Our Story

Like the seeds of the Banksia tree, we sometimes need the stress of a fire to sprout. A dark edge to push us toward the light. A collapse that leads to a renewal. Our candles are designed to evoke memories of those times — when you had a good time with something dark. And were better for it.

Each signature scent is infused with quality essential oils that are meticulously selected, combined, and hand-poured with love in The Sunset, San Francisco. Hand-carved, raw-edge cork tops entrap the warmth and dim the light of your logo-etched vessel. Please continue the life of your candle after its final ember: the food-safe glass is perfect for cocktails, small storage, or planting.

Ember + Seed is the creative work of Marcus and Alex, with deep support from our frenchton, Baxter.
