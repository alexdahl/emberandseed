Our Story

We sometimes need the stress of a fire to sprout. A dark edge to push us toward the light. A collapse that leads to a renewal. Our candles are designed to evoke memories of those times — when you had a good time with something dark. And were better for it.

Ember + Seed is handcrafted in The Sunset, San Francisco, by Marcus and Alex. We’re dedicated to discovering and combining unique elements into premium soy candles.
